Our Luma Calendar

Our shared public calendar is at https://luma.com/fractalboston (or also https://fractal.boston/calendar)

Subscribe on Luma for updates, and also add it to your cal so you can easily follow new events.

Submit Your Event

Go to https://luma.com/fractalboston and click Submit Event.

Once you've created it, the calendar admins will need to approve it. Post in the Discord to ping them.

Recurring Events

Luma doesn't do recurring events but they do give you an easy way to clone an existing event a bunch of times.

Once you've created the first event, go to Manage → More and there's a Clone Event button.

You can choose to make a few at a time, or click Recurrence (below the time inputs) and throw a bunch on there.
